What is this website?

For those of you that have not played it, this lovely game is a metroidvania bullet-hell developed by GemaYue and CreSpirit, published by Sekai Project, PQube, and Circle Entertainment! It's super fun and has difficulties for all types of players, so if you haven't tried it, I'd recommend giving it a go!!

This website is to help aspiring Rabi-ribi players learn how to dodge boss attack patterns without having to scour the internet for a video that demonstrates it for them! It can be useful to pinpoint the exact attack a player is looking for without having to skim a video, hoping that somebody does it without getting hit or needing a forced invincibility frame. This site comes with a loop button to loop the attack for the person, so that they don't constantly need to rewind the same segment over-and-over again! Alternatively, users may also use video controls to pause and play as desired.

For now this site focuses solely on the hardest bosses in the game: SP Rita, SP Syaro, SP Miru, and SP Irisu. In the future I hope to update it to include all bosses at the BEX (Bunny Extinction) difficulty as well. Note: the reason why I don't do any easier difficulty is because attacks are still mostly the same, just perhaps slower or less bullets. Difficulties above BEX are non-standard, and from what I've read and interpreted, it seems that those difficulties aren't made in mind for players to dodge every attack without needing forced invincibility frames; thus, I focused on BEX.
